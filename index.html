Housing Price Prediction Tool for Major US Cities

Housing prices in the United States (US) continue to increase as incomes rise, unemployment drops, and industries grow. By analyzing housing market data and trends between 2015-2019, the Housing Price Prediction Tool will predict whether the housing market will rise or drop in the 50 largest cities in the US.
